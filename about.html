My name is Dylan Jude Chope, I'm based on Gadigal land in Eora/Sydney, and I use he/him pronouns. I'm a guitarist and a drummer, a composer and songwriter, and a woodwind player. I'm currently studying a Bachelor of Music, specialising in composition, at the Sydney Conservatorium of Music.

I play in a few bands gigging in the Sydney scene, playing in Bourke's, Colour Trove, and Black Magic Fairytale.

Non-musically, I'm an Anarchist, an amateur photographer, an urbanist AND a lover of nature, a train foamer, and a glutton for knowledge fascinated by history, archaeology and geography.
